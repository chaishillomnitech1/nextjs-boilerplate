2. **Access Control**: Biometric authentication, multi-factor access
3. **Monitoring**: 24/7 surveillance, automated threat detection
4. **Response**: Local and remote security teams, law enforcement partnerships

#### Maintenance Protocols
1. **Preventive Maintenance**: Regular inspections and servicing
2. **Predictive Maintenance**: IoT sensors and AI-driven predictions
3. **Emergency Repairs**: On-site spare parts, rapid response teams
4. **Documentation**: Complete maintenance logs and asset registries

#### Environmental Controls

## Risk Assessment & Mitigation

### High Priority Risks

1. **Geopolitical Instability**
   - Risk: Political changes affecting compound operations
   - Mitigation: Geographic diversity, flexible partnerships, insurance

2. **Regulatory Changes**
   - Risk: New regulations limiting operations
   - Mitigation: Proactive compliance, legal teams, lobbying efforts

3. **Technology Obsolescence**
   - Risk: Rapid technology changes
   - Mitigation: Modular design, upgrade plans, R&D investment

4. **Cybersecurity Threats**
   - Risk: Attacks on network infrastructure
   - Mitigation: Defense-in-depth, monitoring, incident response, insurance

5. **Financial Market Volatility**
   - Risk: Cryptocurrency market crashes
   - Mitigation: Diversified revenue, stablecoin reserves, traditional finance

### Medium Priority Risks

6. **Natural Disasters**
   - Risk: Earthquakes, floods, etc. damaging compounds
   - Mitigation: Site selection, hardening, insurance, redundancy

7. **Key Person Dependencies**
   - Risk: Loss of critical team members
   - Mitigation: Documentation, succession planning, distributed leadership

8. **Partnership Failures**
   - Risk: Partners fail to deliver or exit
   - Mitigation: Multiple partners per region, contract protections

9. **Community Backlash**
   - Risk: Local opposition to compounds
   - Mitigation: Community engagement, local benefits, transparency

10. **Budget Overruns**
    - Risk: Costs exceed projections
    - Mitigation: Contingency reserves, phased deployment, value engineering
