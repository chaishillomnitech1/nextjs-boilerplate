## Risk Mitigation

### Supply Chain Risks
- **Component shortages**: Multi-sourcing strategy, advance ordering
- **Shipping delays**: Buffer time in schedules, regional suppliers
- **Vendor bankruptcy**: Diversified vendor base, escrow for critical equipment

### Technical Risks
- **Equipment failure**: Comprehensive warranties, spare parts, redundancy
- **Technology obsolescence**: Modular design, upgrade paths
- **Integration challenges**: Thorough testing, experienced integrators

### Geopolitical Risks
- **Export restrictions**: Compliance team, alternative suppliers
- **Sanctions**: Country risk assessment, contingency plans
- **Access restrictions**: Multiple routing options, satellite backup
